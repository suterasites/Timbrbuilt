FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What areas do you cover?

We work across Melbourne's inner west and Hobsons Bay, including Williamstown, Newport, Yarraville, Spotswood, Seddon, Altona and the surrounding suburbs. If you are just outside that, get in touch and we will let you know.

Do you charge for a quote?

No. Every quote starts with a free on-site measure so we can see the job properly, then you get a fixed written price with no obligation.

What kind of work do you take on?

Carpentry and building work: framing and structural carpentry, bathroom renovations, decks and pergolas, and new builds through to lockup. If you are not sure whether your job fits, just ask.

Will I get a fixed price or an estimate?

A fixed written quote. We price the job up front so there are no mid-job surprises, and the price holds from quote to handover.

Who actually does the work?

Timbr Built is hands-on, so the qualified carpenter who quotes your job is the one on the tools. Where a job needs other trades, we coordinate them so you have one point of contact.

How do I get started?

Call Luca on 0484 698 553 or send your details through the contact form. We will book a time for a free measure and take it from there.
