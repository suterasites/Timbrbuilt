## Brand palette (see styles.css `:root`)
- Navy `#16293A` (from the wordmark) - primary, headings, dark sections
- Navy-900 `#0E1A26` - hero/footer/CTA backgrounds
- Timber `#B08553` - warm accent (eyebrows, rules, icons), used sparingly
- Surface `#F6F6F3`, Stone `#ECEBE5`, lines `#E3E2DB`

## Notes
- `sips` on this Mac could not encode WebP, so photos are optimized JPEG. If a WebP encoder
  becomes available, re-export for a further ~30% saving.
- More/newer photos can be dropped in and referenced the same way (keep them portrait-friendly
  or they'll crop in the 4:3 / 3:4 frames).
