About Timbr Built

A local carpenter who backs the work.

Timbr Built is a qualified carpenter based in Melbourne's inner west, taking on the jobs that make a house feel like home, from framing and new builds to bathrooms, decks and everyday carpentry.

Our approach

Trade-trained, hands-on, and easy to deal with.

Timbr Built is a hands-on business, so the person quoting your job is the person doing it. That means straight answers, a price that holds, and a standard of work a carpenter is happy to put a name to.

Whether it's a full bathroom renovation, a new deck for summer, or framing a new build through to lockup, the job is planned properly, communicated clearly, and finished clean.

What we stand for

Values on every job.

Do it once, right

Quality carpentry that lasts, not a quick patch. The frame gets the same care as the finish, because both decide how long the work holds up.

Straight talk, fixed price

A clear scope and a fixed written price before we start, so you always know where things stand and there are no mid-job surprises.

One team, managed

The person who quotes your job is the one on the tools, coordinating every trade so you have a single point of contact from start to finish.

Local and reliable

Based in the inner west, turning up when we say we will and leaving the site swept clean at the end of every day.
