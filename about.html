About

A local carpenter who
backs the work.

Timbr Built is a qualified carpenter based in Melbourne's inner west, taking on the jobs that make a house feel like home, from framing and new builds to bathrooms, decks and everyday carpentry.

Our approach

Trade-trained, hands-on, and easy to deal with.

Timbr Built is a hands-on business, so the person quoting your job is the person doing it. That means straight answers, a price that holds, and a standard of work a carpenter is happy to put a name to.

Whether it's a full bathroom renovation, a new deck for summer, or framing a new build through to lockup, the job is planned properly, communicated clearly, and finished clean.
